Maison Bags — Website Plan

A modern, elegant online store selling women’s handbags.

Site Name

Maison Bags

Reason for this name

“Maison” means “house” in French and is often used in luxury fashion brands. It communicates elegance, style, and modern boutique aesthetics — perfect for a women’s handbag store.

Site Purpose

Maison Bags will serve as a clean, modern shopping experience where users can:

  • Browse the online catalog of handbags
  • View deals and special collections
  • Learn about the brand and contact the store
  • Submit a contact form for questions

Target Audience Scenarios

  • “I want to see the available styles and prices of handbags.”
  • “I want to check if there are deals or discounts this week.”
  • “I want to contact the store to ask about shipping or deliveries.”

Color Scheme
